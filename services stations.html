SERVICES AT STATION




Cloak rooms and safety lockers are available at major stations where luggage can be kept in safe custody

Passengers can contact the train conductors/coach attendants or other authorised catering staff of Railway for availing the catering service.

In case of any medical emergency, passenger can approach the travelling ticket examiner/train superintendent







Bed-rolls are supplied to passengers in AC First Class, AC 2-Tier and AC 3-Tier Classes in all trains

In case of any medical emergency, passenger can approach the travelling ticket examiner/train superintendent

The Indian Railways also offers free public Wi-Fi facility - RailWire - to rail users at over 700 railway stations
